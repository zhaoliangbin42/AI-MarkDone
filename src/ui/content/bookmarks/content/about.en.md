# About

AI-MarkDone started as a reading and capture tool I built for myself. AI chat products keep getting more capable, but long-form reading, preserving structure, copying source content, and organizing what matters still feel rough in everyday use. I did not want to build a second app on top of those pages. I wanted the truly high-frequency, high-value workflows to feel natural inside the chat surfaces people already use.

## Why I built AI-MarkDone

I increasingly use AI conversations as a working surface, not just a temporary Q&A window. The problem is that most host pages are designed for chatting, not necessarily for reading, revising, or archiving. AI-MarkDone grew out of that gap: I wanted important content to be calmer to read, easier to save, more faithful to copy, and faster to send back into another model when the next step is refinement.

## Principles I keep coming back to

- Stay close to the host product instead of turning the page into a different app.
- Preserve structure and source whenever possible, not just the rendered surface text.
- Keep advanced capabilities intuitive enough that people can use them without learning a mini system first.
