# Privacy Policy

AI-Markdone does **not collect, store, or transmit any user data**.

- ❌ No personal information
- ❌ No conversation content

## How It Works

All processing happens **locally in your browser**:
- Content parsing occurs on your device
- No data is sent to external servers
- No network requests are made

- **clipboardWrite**: Copy content when you click copy buttons
- **host permissions**: Inject UI on ChatGPT pages only
